\section{Table with Caption}

Table \ref{table:data} shows how to add a table caption and reference a table.
\begin{table}[h!]
	\centering
	\begin{tabular}{||c c c c||} 
		\hline
		Col1 & Col2 & Col2 & Col3 \\ [0.5ex] 
		\hline\hline
		1 & 6 & 87837 & 787 \\ 
		2 & 7 & 78 & 5415 \\
		3 & 545 & 778 & 7507 \\
		4 & 545 & 18744 & 7560 \\
		5 & 88 & 788 & 6344 \\ [1ex] 
		\hline
	\end{tabular}
	\caption{Table to test captions and labels.}
	\label{table:data}
\end{table}
